毫米波通感实时感知与预警算法方案

I. 系统模型

参数含义
Index | Total | Meaning
N | | 子载波数
L | | 快拍数(frame length)
Ntx | | 发射天线数
Nr | | 接收天线数

A. 发射模型
发射信号:
(1) $\tilde{x}(t)\triangleq\sum_{i=0}^{N_{s}-1}\sum_{l=0}^{L-1}x_{i}[l]e^{j2\pi i\Delta ft}rect(\frac{t-lT}{T})$

B. 通信模型
(2) $x_{i}[l]=W_{i}s_{i}[l]\in\mathbb{C}^{N_{tx}\times1}$
(3) $y_{i,k}[l]\triangleq h_{i,k}^{H}x_{i}[l]+z_{i,k}[l]$

C. 感知模型
(4) $\tilde{y}(t)=\sum_{q=1}^{Q}\beta_{q}b(\phi_{q},\psi_{q})a^{H}(\phi_{q},\psi_{q})\tilde{x}(t-\frac{2R_{q}}{c})e^{j2\pi f_{D,q}t}+\tilde{z}(t)$

其中
(5) $\gamma(\phi_{q},\psi_{q})=[1,...,e^{-j\frac{2\pi}{\lambda_{c}}((N_{rx,x}-1)d_{rx,x}\sin\psi_{q}\cos\phi_{q}+(N_{rx,y}-1)d_{rx,y}\sin\psi_{q}\sin\phi_{q})}]^{T}$
(6) $a(\phi_{q},\psi_{q})=[1,...,e^{j\frac{2\pi}{\lambda_{c}}((N_{tx,x}-1)d_{tx,x}\sin\psi_{q}\cos\phi_{q}+(N_{tx,y}-1)d_{tx,y}\sin\psi_{q}\sin\phi_{q})}]^{T}$

采样后做 DFT 得到:
(7) $y_{i}[l]=\sum_{q=1}^{Q}\beta_{q}b(\phi_{q},\psi_{q})a^{H}(\phi_{q},\psi_{q})x_{i}[l]e^{-j4\pi i\Delta fR_{q}/c}e^{j4\pi lTv_{q}f_{c}/c}+z_{i}[l]$
(8) $=\sum_{q=1}^{Q}\beta_{q}b(\phi_{q},\psi_{q})a^{H}(\phi_{q},\psi_{q})x_{i}[l]e^{ji\omega_{r}(R_{q})}e^{jl\omega_{v}(v_{q})}+z_{i}[l]$

其中
(9) $\omega_{r}(R_{q})=-4\pi\Delta fR_{q}/c$
(10) $\omega_{v}(v_{q})=4\pi Tv_{q}f_{c}/c$

考虑共址的自收自发:
(11) $y_{i}[l]=\sum_{q=1}^{Q}\beta_{q}b(\phi_{q},\psi_{q})a^{H}(\phi_{q},\psi_{q})x_{i}[l]e^{ji\omega_{r}(R_{q})}e^{jl\omega_{v}(v_{q})}+\sqrt{\rho_{SI}}H_{SI}[i]x_{i}[l]+z_{i}[l]$

其中
(12) $H_{SI}[i]=\sqrt{\frac{\kappa_{SI}}{\kappa_{SI}+1}}H_{SI}^{LoS}[i]+\sqrt{\frac{1}{\kappa_{SI}+1}}H_{SI}^{NLoS}[i]$
